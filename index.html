Problem Statement

Globally, 466 million deaf people face language barriers that affect education and employment.

Most deaf children are born to hearing parents and lack written sign language, resulting in a severe vocabulary gap.

As a result, deaf students often read at a 3rd-4th grade level and face high dropout rates.

Only 3% of deaf children globally have access to bilingual education.
